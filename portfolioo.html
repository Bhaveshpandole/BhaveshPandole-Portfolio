Edufaith

Quiz Game

I have developed the Edufaith Quiz Game,a fully dynamic website designed to make learning interactive and fun. The game covers a total 8 subjects, and each subject contain 10 MCQ. Once the quiz begins,users cannot return to previous options,ensuring a fair and forward-moving challenge. At the end of each quiz,the system automatically evaluates the answers and displays the result out of 10.
